About Us

started in 1935 as a missionary station to combat deadly tropical diseases in the colonial Kenya.We have grown in the years having equiped ourselves with state of the art equipment that allow us to provide specified healthcare to all.We are ranked among the best hospitals in the country located along the Nairobi-Nakuru highway.We offer all medical services with a 24/7 open pharmacy and clinics

Our mission

Health for all members of the society irrespective of their social class

Our Vision

Creating a healthy working nation

Our core values

  • providing healthcare to all
  • fulfilling universal healthcare
  • Affordable healthcare
  • promoting doctor patient relationship
  • Educating the community on healthy living
